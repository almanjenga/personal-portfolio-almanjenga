About Me

Hi! I’m Alma Mugure, a Bachelor of Science in Informatics and Computer Science student at Strathmore University (2022–2026). I focus on software engineering and have a growing passion for cybersecurity. I’ve made the Dean’s List and enjoy collaborating on projects that create real-world impact in my community.

Beyond the classroom, I’ve led and served in several roles with AIESEC, including Accommodations Manager and Head of Logistics for organizing committees. I’ve also worked with @iLabAfrica as a Data Science Intern and contributed to community initiatives like Linda Watoto and education volunteering in Athi River.

I’m building products that matter. I developed Eunoia, a mental health platform, and I’m working on Tutoria, a peer‑to‑peer learning app for Strathmore students. For my final‑year project, I’m researching a culturally sensitive NLP‑based menstrual health chatbot tailored for Kenyan youth.
